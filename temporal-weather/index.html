Hordaland average temperature range by year

How to read this chart...

  • Average daily temperature is represented along the x-axis. Each circle represents the average temperature of a single day of a year.
  • Data for Hordaland weather was collected over a 10-year period; the years of collection are arranged along the y-axis. This in effect creates a set of temperature bands, which allow us to see outliers in average temperature within and over the entire collection span.
  • Daily temperature circles are color-coded to show type of precipitation on that day. Colors represent the following precipitation types:
    • Off-white: ND
    • Dark orange: RR
    • Light blue: RS
    • Dark blue: SS
    We can see from this color scheme that there is mostly no data collected over this period, except for the first few years of the collection.

Temperature and Rainfall by Year

How to read this chart...

  • Years form the outermost circle -- select one of these years to view the daily average temperature and precipitation (mm) for each day of the calendar year.
  • Average temperature is shown as a small colored dot positioned along the circle axis to indicate mean temperature for that day.
  • Precipitation is encoded with area, indicated by a circle with its center oriented with its associated daily temperature bar. The larger the circle, the greater the amount of precipitation for that day.
